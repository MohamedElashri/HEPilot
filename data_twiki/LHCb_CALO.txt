General information

### Opening your browser to the outside world

The twiki pages below may contain links pointing outside the LHCb online system (EDMS, Indico, ...). When browsing fom the control room PC the outside connection is not allowed by default. However it is possible to add a proxy in the personal configuration of your internet browser to allow the outside connection. Instructions are [here](https://lbtwiki.cern.ch/bin/view/CALO/OpenBrowser).
